Early Blight Detected

Tomato Early Blight (Alternaria solani)

Dark concentric spots on lower leaves suggest early blight. Left untreated, this can spread to stems and fruit, reducing yield by up to 30%.

Recommended Action

  • Remove and destroy affected leaves immediately.
  • Apply a copper-based fungicide every 7-10 days.
  • Improve air circulation by spacing plants further apart.
